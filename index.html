Welcome to Abundant Life
Clinical Psychology

We all know the feeling of just trying to get through another day, or even worse, not wanting to face another day at all. However, at ALCP we believe that there is the possibility for every one of us to live lives full of joy, peace and purpose. Our desire is to help every individual we work with to achieve a life that they genuinely want to live, within which there is fullness and passion.

Rachael Ambler is the owner and sole practitioner at ALCP. She has chosen to maintain a small and personal practice, to ensure a warm and compassionate environment is provided to every single person she works with.

Explore Services

About Rachael

As a clinical psychologist, Rachael specialises in the diagnosis and treatment of a wide range of psychological problems affecting individual adults and adolescents. She has been practising as a psychologist since 1999, and has been working in her own private practice since 2002. Her practice is located in Mt Gravatt, Brisbane.

She has worked in a variety of settings, including schools, medical centres, universities, Lifeline, community clinics, and hospitals.

Rachael strives to provide her clients and supervisees with a supportive and empathic therapeutic environment and is very passionate about her clinical work.

A Little About Rachael

Outside of the practice, Rachael is a mum to two young-adult daughters and two very spoilt dogs (both foster-fails!). She loves dancing, singing, reading, going to the beach, hiking, and being involved in her church community. This warmth and genuine care for people is something her clients experience from the very first session.
